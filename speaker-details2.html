DR. GERD DROESSE

PROFILE

• With over 35 years of experience in working for international organizations

• Served from April 2018 as General Counsel of the World Green Economy Organization (WGEO), in charge of drafting and finalizing the Agreement establishing the World Green Economy Organization (WGEO Agreement) for signature by UN member states and establishing the legal, regulatory and policy framework of WGEO as an international organization

• As Senior Advisor, provided for over three years management and policy advice to the Executive Director and General Counsel of the Green Climate Fund (GCF) and led the negotiations of the GCF with 120 countries on privileges and immunities
